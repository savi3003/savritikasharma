COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS


Filling in forms can be very
OVERWHELMING and TIME CONSUMING.

Most resume apps’ form filling process caused users to give up halfway. My solution
was to make the form similar to a hackathon form with a single prompt on each
page. I also wanted to add a feature where the user could monitor their quick
progression and make the navigation swiping up to make the entire process seem a
lot faster and not overwhelming.
